Mary Dang, your next front end developer

Hello!

My name is Mary Dang and I am a front end developer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I graduated with Bachelor's Degree in Finance in June of 2016. During my last year of college, I started getting into programming by watching videos online and building websites. It wasn't long before that I decided to start pursuing what was at first a learning experience as a career.

Innovative, Rewarding, and Fun

As a finance student, I worked with Excel in every class. I was able to build spreadsheets with formulas and it turned out I was good at this. I decided to start learning programming because SQL is a language finance professionals use but at my college, they didn't offer a SQL course. I joined Treehouse and quickly learned SQL, but it didn't stop there. I learned ruby and built ruby on rails applications, python, and then started getting into front end development. Through developing, I can explore my innovative side when figuring out how to go about a project and problem solving. With every project I complete, I feel rewarded and most importantly, I have fun.
